Why Basketball is So Fun

Basketball is a fast-paced sport that requires skill, teamwork, and strategy. Basketball also has very exciting and nerve racking moments constantly, for example when Lebron blocked Andre iguodala to secure the 2016 championship, or when Kawhi Leonard hit the game winner buzzer beater to win the game. It's exciting to play and watch!

Fun Facts About Basketball

  • An NBA basketball hoop is 10 feet high.
  • The highest-scoring NBA game ever had 370 combined points!
  • The first NBA game was the New York Knicks vs the Toronto Huskies.
  • Kobe Bryant once scored 81 points in a game against the Toronto Raptors
