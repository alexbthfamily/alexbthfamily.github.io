Basketball: The Game We Love

About Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced sport played between two teams of five players each. The goal is to score points by shooting the ball into the opposing team's hoop.

Rules of the Game

  • A game consists of four quarters of 12 minutes each (NBA) or 10 minutes (FIBA).
  • Players score by shooting the ball into the opponent’s hoop.
  • Dribbling is required to move with the ball.
  • The team with the most points at the end wins.
